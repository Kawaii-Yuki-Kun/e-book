About this guide

Most buyers inspect a used car for under four minutes. The person selling it has lived with it for years. This guide closes that gap with a calm, ordered procedure you can run in any parking lot — no tools, no scanner, no mechanical background required.

One missed head-gasket, frame repair, or rolled-back odometer costs roughly a thousand times the price of this guide. The math makes the decision for you.

What's inside

  • 11 checkpoints, in the exact order you run them — cheapest checks first, deal-enders before you fall in love with the car.
  • The “walk away now” red-flag list — the conditions that end the conversation.
  • A printable one-page score sheet — mark Pass / Note / Fail at the car.
  • Word-for-word renegotiation scripts — the sheet does the talking.
  • Instant PDF — read on a phone or print. Yours forever.
