CKPT 01 · STANCE
CKPT 01 · Approach

Read the stance.

Before contact, sight the car as one assembly. Ride height even? Panel gaps symmetric? A unit that's been hit hard rarely returns to spec.

FAIL → panel gaps asymmetric L/R
CKPT 02 · Body & paint

Datum the paint.

Sight down each panel in daylight. Overspray, shade shift, or orange-peel on one door = repaint = an undeclared event in the car's history.

FAIL → fresh undercoat over "rust-free"
CKPT 03 · Tires & brakes

The tires are telemetry.

Wear pattern outputs the state of alignment, suspension, and frame. Four tires from four different weeks means maintenance was reactive, not real.

FAIL → inner-edge wear + vague steering
CKPT 04 · Engine bay

Open it cold.

Creamy film under the oil cap = coolant in the wrong circuit. A surgically clean bay on a high-mileage unit usually means a fresh leak was scrubbed off.

FAIL → emulsion under oil cap
CKPT 05 · Interior & VIN

Cross-check the log.

Pedal and wheel wear must agree with the odometer. Every VIN on the car must match the title — verified before price is discussed.

FAIL → VIN plate shows tampering
CKPT 06 · Test drive

Run it under load.

Cold start, windows down, audio off. One hard brake on an empty road. Hands light on a straight. Under load, the system reports its real condition.

FAIL → pull, shudder, or knock under load
