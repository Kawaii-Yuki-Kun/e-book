Before you ask

Reasonable questions

Do I need tools or experience?
No. The whole guide is built around what you can check with your eyes, your hands, a phone flashlight, and a short test drive. No jack, no scanner, no mechanical background assumed.
Is it specific to one make or country?
The checkpoints are universal — they're about how cars physically wear, not a particular brand. Document and title specifics vary by region; the guide explains what to verify so you can apply it locally.
What format is it, and how do I get it?
A PDF you can read on a phone or print as a one-page sheet to carry. After checkout you get an instant download — no account, no app.
What if it doesn't help me?
7-day refund, no questions. If running the checkpoints once doesn't make you more confident at the curb, you shouldn't pay for it.
